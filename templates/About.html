Our Story

Inspired by the challenges faced by farmers in combating crop diseases, we founded Kheti.Ai. Our team of passionate experts and technologists came together to develop a solution that could revolutionize the way crop health is monitored and managed. Driven by a commitment to sustainable agriculture, we've dedicated ourselves to creating a platform that empowers farmers with accurate disease detection and effective prevention strategies

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers with cutting-edge technology to ensure healthy and sustainable crop yields. We leverage advanced machine learning to accurately detect crop diseases, providing timely and actionable insights. By offering effective prevention and cure methods, we aim to contribute to a more resilient and productive agricultural ecosystem.

Who We Are

We are a team of dedicated professionals passionate about advancing agricultural technology. With expertise in machine learning, data science, and agriculture, we've developed a cutting-edge platform designed to assist farmers in safeguarding their crops from diseases. Our mission is to empower farmers with the knowledge and tools they need to ensure healthy and thriving harvests.
